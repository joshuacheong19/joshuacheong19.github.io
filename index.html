01  ·  Systematic Commodities
Chinese Soybean Crush Margin Mean Reversion Signal
Python · Bloomberg BQL · BQuant · DCE Soybean Meal

A regime-filtered systematic signal for DCE soybean meal futures built on Chinese crusher economics. Computes daily Brazilian landed-cost parity across CBOT, DCE, FX, and FOB premiums spanning seven US-China tariff regimes. A port stock regime filter — keyed on the 12-week change in Chinese imported soybean inventory — resolves a complete signal breakdown during the 2024–2025 oversupply period, producing positive returns in every calendar year.

02  ·  Sell-Side Data Infrastructure
TP Revision & Rating Tracker
Python · Bloomberg BQL · BQuant · ipywidgets

A daily sell-side target price and rating revision scanner using Bloomberg BQL's per-broker best_target_price time series. Detects intraday broker-level TP changes with upgrade/downgrade classification, filtering by a minimum move threshold. Outputs a sortable table showing previous and new TP, % change, spot price, consensus TP, and an inline SVG 90-day TP distribution strip per stock — across US, SG, HK, and TH universes.
